What makes Alex different?

Single

Alex is a unified enterprise metadata management platform, as opposed to a collection of disparate products. We give you everything you need to build a single source of truth for data in the enterprise out of the box.

Simple

If you’ve used a social media site, you can use Alex. Our design philosophy is one of accessibility, enabling both business and technical users to reduce risk and add value.

Social

Alex is built to facilitate collaboration around data. Our suite of social features allow individuals and teams to work together and make data and insights shareable and actionable.

How we do it

Alex harvests the richest metadata from the widest range of technologies in order to support your entire enterprise data system.

About us

Alex was born from subject matter expertise and the desire to develop a solution to complex industry challenges in enterprise data management.

Our name is a reference to the Ancient Library of Alexandria – the keeper of all knowledge. Today, Alex is a diverse team of professionals working to make your data the best it can be.
